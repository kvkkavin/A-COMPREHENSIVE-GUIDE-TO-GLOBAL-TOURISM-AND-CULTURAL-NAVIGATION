•	 Cultural Oversimplification: There is a risk of oversimplifying complex cultural narratives and reducing diverse cultures to stereotypes or clichés, undermining the guide's effectiveness in promoting cultural understanding. This can result in strained interactions, cultural misunderstandings, and negative experiences for both travelers and locals.
